## Phase 1 Hires (Months 1–4)

### Hire 1: ML / NLP Engineer
- **Start:** Month 1
- **Why now:** VoiceNLP development begins Phase 1; wake-word and intent classification require dedicated ML expertise. CTO cannot own this and build the agent architecture simultaneously.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years NLP; experience with on-device / edge model deployment (not cloud-only); ideally prior work with noisy-environment audio
- **Comp:** $140–160K base + equity

### Hire 2: UX Researcher
- **Start:** Month 1
- **Why now:** 10 contextual interviews must be completed by Month 2. A PM conducting interviews solo cannot maintain observation rigor while facilitating.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years UX research; experience with contextual inquiry in industrial / field settings; automotive or manufacturing domain a plus
- **Comp:** $110–130K base + equity

### Hire 3: Mechanical / Embedded Systems Engineer
- **Start:** Month 2
- **Why now:** Edge server installation in the service bay, ArUco marker calibration, OBD-II physical integration — all require hardware engineering competence that is different from software.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years embedded systems or automotive hardware; CAN bus / OBD-II experience strongly preferred; comfortable in a service bay environment
- **Comp:** $130–150K base + equity

### Hire 4: Legal Counsel (Part-time / Contract)
- **Start:** Month 1
- **Why now:** OEM CAD licensing negotiations begin Month 1. Outside IP and contract counsel needed — cannot use a general-purpose startup attorney for OEM agreements this size.
- **Requirements:** IP licensing experience; automotive OEM contracts preferred; OSHA/SAE familiarity a plus
- **Structure:** Contract / outside counsel; target firm with automotive OEM experience (Foley & Lardner, Honigman)

## Phase 2 Hires (Months 5–8)

### Hire 5: Senior Software Engineer — Spatial Computing
- **Start:** Month 5
- **Why now:** DiagnosticCore, CADRenderer, SensorFusion all being built simultaneously. CTO needs a senior engineer who owns the HoloLens 2 spatial computing layer (SLAM, ArUco, rendering pipeline).
- **Requirements:** 5+ years software engineering; HoloLens 2 / Mixed Reality Toolkit (MRTK) experience required; 3D rendering and SLAM experience preferred
- **Comp:** $160–185K base + equity

### Hire 6: QA / Safety Engineer
- **Start:** Month 5
- **Why now:** Phase 2 exit criterion includes 50-scenario HV-STOP safety audit. A dedicated QA engineer must own test matrix development, execution, and documentation — this cannot be developer-tested.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years QA in safety-critical systems; FMEA / hazard analysis experience; automotive or medical device background preferred
- **Comp:** $120–140K base + equity

### Hire 7: Data Engineer
- **Start:** Month 6
- **Why now:** Telemetry pipeline, VIN-indexed CAD cache, and latency benchmarking infrastructure all need dedicated data engineering. This is not product feature work — it's the data foundation every metric depends on.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years data engineering; AWS (Kinesis, S3, Athena, Glue); time-series data experience a plus (relevant for predictive maintenance Phase 5)
- **Comp:** $130–150K base + equity

### Hire 8: Technical Writer / Documentation Lead
- **Start:** Month 7
- **Why now:** OEM compliance submissions (SAE J1742, OSHA 1910.147) require formal technical documentation that engineers cannot produce at the required quality while building product.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years technical writing in regulated industry (automotive, medical, aerospace); familiarity with SAE standards preferred
- **Comp:** $90–110K base + equity

## Phase 3 Hires (Months 9–14)

### Hire 9: Field Install / Solutions Engineer
- **Start:** Month 9
- **Why now:** Phase 3 deploys to 3 real dealerships. Field install requires a dedicated engineer who handles bay prep, edge server installation, headset provisioning, and network configuration on-site.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years field engineering or solutions engineering; networking (WiFi6E, firewall config); comfortable working in automotive service bays
- **Comp:** $110–130K base + equity

### Hire 10: Customer Success Manager (CSM)
- **Start:** Month 9
- **Why now:** First paying beta sites need a dedicated CSM. Onboarding, activation, and health score monitoring are full-time work for 3 accounts with high-touch requirements.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years CS in B2B SaaS; automotive dealer tech preferred (CDK, Reynolds, Dealertrack experience valuable); data-driven (comfortable using dashboards)
- **Comp:** $90–110K base + equity

### Hire 11: Compliance Manager
- **Start:** Month 10
- **Why now:** SAE J1742 submission and OSHA 1910.147 audit readiness both require dedicated compliance management. Outside counsel handles legal review; internal compliance manager drives the process.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years compliance in automotive, industrial, or safety-regulated environment; OSHA knowledge required; SAE familiarity preferred
- **Comp:** $100–120K base + equity

### Hire 12: Product Designer (UX/UI)
- **Start:** Month 11
- **Why now:** Phase 3 introduces manager dashboard, confidence survey, job card integration — the most UI-heavy features to date. A dedicated designer is needed; UX Researcher cannot own interaction design.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years product design; spatial / AR UI experience a plus; design systems experience (the headset UX needs consistency across all features)
- **Comp:** $120–140K base + equity

### Hire 13: Account Executive (AE) #1
- **Start:** Month 12
- **Why now:** Phase 4 GA is 3 months away. AE needs time to learn the product, build pipeline, and close first deals before Month 15 launch.
- **Requirements:** 5+ years B2B SaaS sales in automotive dealer tech; existing dealer group relationships; comfortable with 90-day sales cycles
- **Comp:** $80K base + $120K OTE + equity

### Hire 14: Account Executive (AE) #2
- **Start:** Month 13
- **Why now:** Two AEs needed to hit 50-bay Phase 4 target; pipeline builds over 6 months.
- **Same profile and comp as AE #1**

## Phase 4 Hires (Months 15–20)

### Hire 15: VP of Engineering
- **Start:** Month 15
- **Why now:** Engineering team is 8 people at Phase 4 start. CTO needs to shift from IC work to architecture and external relationships (OEM technical partnerships). VP Engineering manages day-to-day eng team.
- **Requirements:** 8+ years engineering; 3+ years engineering management; spatial computing or automotive domain preferred; experience scaling 10→30 person eng teams
- **Comp:** $200–240K base + equity

### Hire 16: Security Engineer
- **Start:** Month 15 (Month 20 at latest for SOC 2 obs period)
- **Why now:** SOC 2 Type II observation period begins Month 22; security controls must be in place 6 months prior. Security Engineer owns the control environment.
- **Requirements:** 4+ years security engineering; AWS security (GuardDuty, IAM, KMS); SOC 2 experience required; penetration testing experience a plus
- **Comp:** $150–175K base + equity

### Hire 17–18: 2 Additional CSMs
- **Start:** Month 15–16 (scale with bay count)
- **Why now:** At 50 bays, 1 CSM per 50 bays means 1 CSM needed. Hire 2 to build ahead of Phase 5 ramp.
- **Same profile as Hire 10**

### Hire 19: Marketing Manager
- **Start:** Month 16
- **Why now:** NADA conference is Month 18. Case study publication, conference materials, and website content require a dedicated marketing owner.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years B2B marketing; content and event marketing; automotive dealer industry knowledge preferred
- **Comp:** $100–120K base + equity

### Hire 20–22: 3 Software Engineers (Generalist)
- **Start:** Month 16–18 (staggered)
- **Why now:** Phase 4 feature work (RLHF pipeline, Spanish NLP, multi-OEM handoff) alongside Phase 5 preparation requires additional engineering headcount.
- **Requirements:** 3+ years software engineering; Python/TypeScript; ML systems or distributed systems experience
- **Comp:** $130–155K base + equity
